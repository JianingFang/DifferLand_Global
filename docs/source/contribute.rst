Contribution and Collaboration
==============================

We welcome contributions to the **DifferLand** project!  
Our goal is to build a community around advancing differentiable modeling of the terrestrial ecosystem.

Opportunities for contribution include:

* **Adding new modules** – e.g. alternative spatialization architectures or process-based components.  
* **Expanding process descriptions** – improving mechanistic representation of carbon, water, and energy cycles.  
* **Including new observational constraints** – assimilating diverse datasets (remote sensing, flux towers, inventories) to strengthen model calibration.  

The DifferLand package is publicly available and may be used with proper attribution of the source. If DifferLand represents a substantial component of your research, we warmly encourage you to contact us to discuss your use case and explore opportunities for collaboration.
